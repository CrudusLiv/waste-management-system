Disposal Instructions

 Learn proper waste disposal methods for household, hazardous, and electronic waste
home Household Waste

Guidelines for disposing household waste:

  • Use designated bins
  • Separate recyclables
  • Compact waste when possible
  • Follow local collection schedules
 warning Hazardous Waste

Guidelines for hazardous waste disposal:

  • Special handling required
  • Contact local authorities
  • Use designated facilities
  • Never mix materials
 devices E-Waste

Guidelines for electronic waste disposal:

  • Visit certified recycling centers
  • Secure data wiping required
  • Keep batteries separate
  • Check manufacturer programs
